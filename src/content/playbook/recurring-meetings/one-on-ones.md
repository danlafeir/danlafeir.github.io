Frequency depends on the individual — every 1 to 3 weeks is a reasonable range. Some people need more frequent touchpoints; others operate well with less. Calibrate based on what you're observing, not just what's scheduled.

Frame feedback in the context of [level expectations](/playbook/career-management/expectations) and technical leadership expectations so conversations about growth are anchored to something concrete.

## Developer 1:1

- Team member's agenda first — always
- Review team meeting contributions
- Review task execution (story cards, blockers, pace)
- Collect and review peer feedback
- Side quests — things they're thinking about outside their assigned work

## Tech Lead 1:1

- Team member's agenda first
- Delivery concerns — what's at risk, what needs unblocking
- People concerns — team dynamics, interpersonal friction
- Technical and architecture concerns
- Review priorities and alignment with broader roadmap

## Staff+ 1:1

- Team member's agenda first
- Delivery concerns
- People concerns
- Technical and architecture concerns
- Organizational and system challenges — cross-team friction, structural issues, process gaps
- Review priorities
